For given $X \in S^m_+$, find the optimal value of the following SDP:

$$
\begin{aligned}
&\text{minimize} &&\mathbf{tr}\ V \\
&\text{subject to} &&
\begin{bmatrix}
X &U \\
U &I
\end{bmatrix}
\geq 0, \quad
\begin{bmatrix}
U &X \\
X &V
\end{bmatrix}
\geq 0,
\end{aligned}
$$

with variables $U,V \in S^m$.
